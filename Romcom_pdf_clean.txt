ROM normalization — the technical moat
Three steps, one-time per user, 30 seconds:
1. Baseline capture. Guide user through max-comfort movements. Track min/max
joint angles per landmark.
2. Normalize. Store profile {joint: (min, max)}. Future readings →  (current −
user_min) / (user_max − user_min). A patient with 45° max shoulder flexion and a
healthy user at 180° both map to 0.0, 1.0.
3. Adaptive thresholds. Exercise targets fire at percentages of personal max.
Hysteresis (activate 70%, deactivate 50%) prevents flicker. EMA filter on joint

LiveAvatar Expanded Document
🧠  LiveAvatar: Real-Time Rehab Companion
Concept: A HeyGen LiveAvatar serves as the patient-facing interface for stroke/TBI
rehabilitation — delivering spoken instructions, adaptive guidance, and positive
reinforcement through a real-time human-like presence.
Why a face matters: Stroke/TBI patients frequently have reading deficits, attention
impairment, and emotional dysregulation. A calm, responsive human face is more
accessible and clinically meaningful than text, audio, or pre-recorded video.

How It Works
The avatar is the body. Our LLM is the brain.
Patient speaks/moves → ASR → LLM (clinical prompt layer) → LiveAvatar Lite API → avatar responds
The avatar delivers step-by-step exercise instructions, monitors patient response, then
adapts — simplifying if the patient struggles, celebrating when they succeed.
Reinforcement is specific and grounded ("You held that two seconds — up from one
last session") rather than generic praise.

📲  Photon Spectrum: Daily Recovery Reminders via
iMessage
Concept
Stroke and TBI patients have consistently poor adherence to at-home exercise
regimens — not from lack of motivation, but because they forget, lose track of time, or
have no external nudge. We use Photon Spectrum to send personalized, AI-generated
daily reminders directly to patients via iMessage — a platform they already use,
requiring zero app download or behavior change.
Why iMessage
No new app to learn or download — critical for this population
Higher open rates than email or push notifications
Spectrum delivers messages in under 1 second on Photon's edge network with
99.9% uptime photon
Supports native platform formatting — polls, buttons, structured responses — so
messages feel natural, not robotic photon
How It Works
Scheduler (daily cron) 
    → Pull patient session data (last completed exercise, streak, next goal)
    → LLM generates personalized message
    → Photon Spectrum iMessage API sends to patient's phone
    → Patient replies "ready" or taps a button
    → System logs response, optionally launches LiveAvatar session

does NOT predict motor cortex activation from performing movements. Using it to
show “brain activation during rehab” is scientifically incorrect. Reframe required:
present TRIBE v2 as predicting cortical response to the visual/audio stimulation of the
rehab game, NOT motor cortex during exercise. Anything else is overclaiming and a
healthcare-savvy judge will catch it.
